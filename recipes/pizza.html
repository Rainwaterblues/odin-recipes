Pizza


Pizza is an Italian dish consisting of a flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomato, cheese, and other ingredients, baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.


Ingredients

Dough preparation

  • 1/2 cups (355 ml) warm water
  • package (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 3/4 cups (490g) bread flour
  • tablespoons extra olive oil (omit if cooking pizza in a wood-fired pizza oven)
  • teaspoons salt
  • teaspoon sugar

Steps

  1. Proof the yeast
  2. Make and knead the pizza dough
  3. Let the dough rise
  4. Preheat the pizza stone
  5. Flatten the dough ball, and stretch out into a round
